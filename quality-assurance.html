Research and Development Quality Assurance Process

Peer Programming

For the proof of concept, we paired up for writing code. One person wrote code while another observed and reviewed the code as it is written in. Therefore, both could fully understand how the code works and learned from each other.

Pair Review

While several documents have been made this semester, each work must be reviewed by another member as each member worked on different sections of the documents. There were few reasons for the pair review.

  • Correctness - The most important reason is to ensure that the contents are correct and the team agrees on it.
  • Consistency - The documents might lose consistency as more than one person participated in a same document. It required to be double-checked for consistency.

External Party Assessment

Any work done by the team was assessed by other people from outside of the team so that it can improve the quality of our work.

  • We frequently sent out work to our supervisor and she gave us useful comments on it. Every time our supervisor gave us her feedback on our work, we created another version of the work based on her feedback. This resulted in reducing mistakes and improving work quality.
  • Other open source developer called Paul who are participating UpStage is one of the external parties that ensure the quality of our work. He always joined the client meeting and discussed any issues we had. Any time we got confused about something, he clearly gave us answers. He has checked our work and interrogated us about any decisions. This made the team to think more and learn more. Also, he gave us suggestions when we tried to make a decision as he has more experiences and knowledge than us. His suggestions normally included a lot of things we did not know about, which made us to research and work more in a good way.
