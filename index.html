UpStage An Online Cyberformance Venue

Introduction

UpStage is an open source web-based application where users can participate in online digital performances. This project has been ongoing since 2004 and has had various teams throughout its creation. The main goal for semester one in 2015 was to find replacements for UpStage technologies that were becoming obsolete so we can find solutions to keep UpStage running. This was to be done by determining the current UpStage technologies used for its features and compare with recent technologies, whilst also maintaining the current version 3 code.

UpStage has been worked on by various student teams at AUT for a long time as an ongoing Reasearch and Development project. The leaving members include Siatua who has been working on UpStage since the first semester of 2015 and Alyssa who has contributed to UpStage in one semester as she is capable of completing the required amount of work in semester 2 of 2015. But due to unforseen circumstances, William who had started working with UpStage in the first semester 2015 will have to continue with UpStage one more semester.

Objectives

  • Intiate research on possible architecture designs for the new UpStage product.
  • Resolve the high priority issues and server deployment processes in the existing product.
